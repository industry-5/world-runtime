# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free environment for
everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and reviews.
- Assume good intent and focus feedback on technical content.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to **conduct@world-runtime.dev**.

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning
or removal from project spaces, depending on severity and repetition.
