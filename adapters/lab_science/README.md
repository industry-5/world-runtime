# Lab Science

_Last updated: 2026-03-23 (America/Chicago)_

`adapter-lab-science` shows how World Runtime fits regulated experiment and release workflows where evidence integrity is part of the operating model.

## What This Track Demonstrates

- provenance-heavy scientific decisions
- governed release and deviation handling
- replayable evidence chains
- policy-visible approval paths in regulated work

## Why It Matters

Lab and regulated science workflows often depend on more than outcomes alone. They also depend on whether the process was followed, the evidence was preserved, and exceptions were reviewed explicitly.

## Boundaries

- this public page describes the domain story only
- it does not publish internal milestone history or validation surfaces
- the track stays focused on regulated evidence integrity rather than broad LIMS coverage
